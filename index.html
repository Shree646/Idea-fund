Founder Story

“Main Bharat Sharma hoon, Nagaur (Rajasthan) ka ek chhota vyakti, par sapne bahut bade hain. Bachpan se hi dekha ki logon ke paas bohot ache ideas hote hain, par paisa nahi hota, support nahi hota. Meri maa kehti thi: 'Beta, soch ko kabhi chhota mat samajhna, har soch ek din duniya badal sakti hai.' Jab main Nagaur ki galiyaan me ghoomta tha, dekhta tha ki chote dukandaar, yuva sabke sapno me jaan hoti hai. Bas unko ek mauka chahiye. Isi liye maine IdeaFund shuru kiya — taki har Nagaur, Rajasthan, aur Bharat ke kone kone se naye ideas ko support mil sake. Aaj main chahta hoon ki aap sab apni soch ko saamne lao, aur milke naye Bharat ka sapna poora karein.”
