Founder Story

“Main Bharat Sharma hoon, Nagaur (Rajasthan) ka rehne wala. Main chhoti si jagah se aata hoon, par mere sapne hamesha bade rahe. Bachpan me dekha ki hamare aas-paas kitne logon ke paas zabardast ideas hote hain, par unke paas wo support nahi hota jo unhe aage badha sake. Main chahta hoon koi bhi idea paison ki kami se mar na jaye. Isi wajah se IdeaFund bana — har idea ko pankh milte hain. Hum paisa nahi, ek saathi, mentor aur support system dete hain. Hamara mission hai: ‘Every dream deserves a chance.’ Aaj main garv se keh sakta hoon ki Nagaur se lekar poore Rajasthan tak, humne kai sapno ko hakikat banaya hai. Aur yeh bas shuruaat hai!”
